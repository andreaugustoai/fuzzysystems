## Technique 2: "What If" Scenarios - Bold Website Possibilities

### What if the website could diagnose AI readiness in 30 seconds?

**Diagnostic Questions to Reveal AI Opportunities:**
- How many tasks have been automated in the past months?
- How many AI agents are currently working for them right now?
- Do they know exactly what people are doing right now so they can see what can be automated?
- What % of your employee time is spent on repetitive data entry?
- How many customer inquiries do you handle daily? (reveals chatbot/support AI potential)
- How long does it take to generate your monthly reports? (reveals analytics AI opportunity)
- Do you have unstructured data (emails, documents, calls) you're not analyzing?

**Industry-Specific Probes:**
- How many invoices/contracts do you process monthly? (document processing AI)
- What's your customer churn prediction accuracy? (predictive AI potential)
- How many hours spent on scheduling/coordination weekly? (workflow automation)

**The Killer Question:**
- What would you do with 20% more capacity without hiring?

### Game-Changing Instant Prototypes Available on Website

**1. Email Intelligence Agent (Most Universal)**
- User uploads 10 sample emails
- AI instantly categorizes, extracts action items, and shows automation potential
- Demonstrates: "Your team spends 3 hours/day on email. We can reduce it to 30 minutes"
- Works for ANY company size

**2. Document Data Extractor**
- Drag & drop any invoice, contract, or form
- Watch AI extract all key data in seconds
- Shows structured output ready for their systems
- Instant wow factor - especially for companies drowning in paperwork

**3. Customer Sentiment Analyzer**
- Paste 10 customer reviews/support tickets
- Get instant sentiment analysis, topic clustering, and priority issues
- Shows hidden patterns they've never seen
- Powerful for companies with customer feedback they're not using

**4. Process Mining Visualizer**
- Upload a simple CSV of any workflow (support tickets, orders, etc.)
- AI creates instant process map showing bottlenecks
- Highlights exactly where AI can cut time/costs
- Visual and immediately actionable

**5. Personal AI Assistant Builder**
- Answer 5 questions about a repetitive task
- Get a working chatbot that handles that specific task
- Can test it immediately in the browser
- They leave with a working AI they built in 2 minutes

**The Game-Changer: "AI ROI Calculator with Live Demo"**
- Based on their diagnostic answers, calculate real ROI
- Then PROVE IT by running their data through a mini prototype
- Show actual time saved on THEIR specific use case
- Display cost savings in real dollars based on their company size

## Technique 4: First Principles - CNPJ Magic

### ALL Available Data from CNPJ:

**Company Core Data:**
- Razão Social (Company Name)
- Natureza Jurídica (Legal Nature)
- Capital Social (Share Capital)
- Porte da Empresa (Company Size: Micro, Small, Other)
- Data de Início (Start Date - company age!)

**Establishment Details:**
- Nome Fantasia (Trade Name)
- Situação Cadastral (Active, Suspended, Closed)
- CNAE Principal & Secundários (Main & Secondary Business Activities)
- Complete Address (Street, Number, Neighborhood, City, State, ZIP)
- Phone Numbers & Email
- Matriz/Filial (Headquarters or Branch)

**Partnership Structure:**
- Sócios (Partners names and roles)
- Qualificação dos Sócios (Partner qualifications)
- Data de Entrada (When partners joined)
- Faixa Etária (Age range of partners)

**Tax Regime:**
- Simples Nacional (Yes/No)
- MEI Status (Yes/No)
- Dates of regime changes

### The CNPJ Instant Intelligence Magic:

**What we can instantly show:**

1. **"We already know your business"**
   - Your industry: [CNAE description]
   - Company age: [X years of experience]
   - Size: [Micro/Small/Medium/Large]
   - Location: [City, State]

2. **"Your AI opportunity score: 87/100"**
   Based on:
   - Your CNAEs show high automation potential
   - Companies your size save R$[X]/month
   - Your industry leaders use AI for [specific tasks]

3. **"Personalized AI Solutions for [Company Name]"**
   - CNAE 4120-4 (Construction)? → "AI for project timeline optimization"
   - CNAE 4771-7 (Retail)? → "AI for inventory prediction"
   - Multiple CNAEs? → "Cross-department AI integration"

4. **"Your competitors are already saving time"**
   - "[X] companies in [your city] with similar CNAEs use AI"
   - "Average time saved: [Y] hours/week"

5. **Smart Insights:**
   - Company age > 10 years? → "Modernize legacy processes"
   - Simples Nacional? → "AI solutions that fit your tax regime"
   - Multiple locations? → "AI to connect your branches"
   - Young partners (Faixa Etária)? → "Tech-forward solutions"
